Who We Are

Warrent Buffet founded Berkshire Hathaway, as a holding company, in 1970 and is still the current CEO/Chairman. Berkshire Hathaway wholly owns companies such as Duracell, GEICO, Fruit of the Loom and large shareholders in multiple airlines, making it one of the largest conglomerate holding companies in the world. Berkshire Hathaway not only does business in the US but around the globe as well.

Our Impact

Berkshire Hathaway Energy's core principle of Environmental RESPECT recognizes the importance of minimizing the impacts our operations have on the environment, and we are striving to achieve net zero greenhouse gas emissions in a manner our customers can afford, our regulators will allow and technology advances support. Through year-end 2021, our businesses had invested more than $30 billion in owned wind, solar and geothermal energy projects.
